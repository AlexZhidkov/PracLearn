FAQ: Questions that the STUDENTS might ask…. Topic: Projects What is a project? Great question! So, a project is basically like a practical subject/unit, we’ve structured our projects to fit within your curriculum so you can actually get work experience as part of your degree. something that you work towards to achieve a particular outcome. In this case, it will be a collaborative enterprise between yourself and the small to medium sized business that you are connected with. For example, the business may require a marketing student to create, manage and grow their social media pages, this could be an example of a project. How long do the projects run for? Will I receive support from my university during the project? Who will be my point of contact during my project? Will I get paid for the project/s? My Projects This lists the all projects that you have applied for. If you haven’t applied for a project, or would like to apply for another project, click the ‘apply for projects’ button. If you have already contacted a small to medium sized business and have created your own project, please click ‘bring my own’ and follow the prompts. If you have any further questions about the projects - please see our FAQ page. Project Feedback This refers to the feedback you receive from the small to medium sized business throughout your internship. You will receive feedback halfway through your internship (30 hour feedback) and then at the end of your internship (final feedback).
